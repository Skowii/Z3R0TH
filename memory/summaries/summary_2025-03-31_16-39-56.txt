This is a library called Customer handling for PHP applications. It's available on sylius.com and has a MIT license. The authors include Paweł Jędrzejewski, Michał Marcinkowski, Łukasz Chruściel, Sylius project, and Community contributions. It requires PHP 7.4 or 8.0 and other dependencies including Doctrine/collections and Sylius/resource. The library uses a variety of development tools like PhpSpec for testing and has some specific folder layouts for its autoload setup.
