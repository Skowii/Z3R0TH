The Sylius/Order library is a PHP management tool for handling orders, sales, shops, webshops, and ecommerce. It is licensed under the MIT license, created by Paweł Jędrzejewski, the Sylius project, and community contributions. Requirements include PHP version 7.4 or 8.0, Sylius/Resource, Webmozart/Assert, and Laminas/Laminas-Stdlib. The library is available at https://sylius.com.
