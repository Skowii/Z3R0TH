Sylius/api-bundle is a Symfony bundle used for API management in Symfony projects. It supports REST and has the MIT license. The bundle is developed by Paweł Jędrzejewski, the Sylius project, and community contributions. Requirements include PHP 7.4 or 8.0, Doctrine/dbal, Api-platform/core, Lexik/jwt-authentication-bundle, Sylius/core-bundle, and Symfony/messenger. Development dependencies consist of Matthiasnoback/symfony-config-test, Nelmio/Alice, Phpspec/phpspec, PHPUnit/phpunit, Symfony/browser-kit, Symfony/debug-bundle, Symfony/dotenv, Symfony/http-client, and Theofidry/alice-data-fixtures. Conflicts are present with doctrine/doctrine-bundle and symfony/doctrine-bridge.
