The 30 Seconds of Code project is community-supported and you can contribute by submitting issues or pull requests related to bugs, improvements, or typos. Be polite and follow the provided guidelines while making changes to the website content. Content should be written in American spelling, with short sentences, active voice, and non-directional language. Capitalize the first letter of a sentence, keep original capitalization for terminology and acronyms, avoid ampersands and use quotation marks for defining words or quoted text.
