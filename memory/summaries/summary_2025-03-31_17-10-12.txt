The Sylius/LocaleBundle is a Symfony bundle that provides locales management for Symfony projects. It enables language, localization, and internationalization in the project. The bundle requires PHP version 7.4 or 8.0, and dependencies include Sylius/Locale, Sylius/ResourceBundle, and Symfony/FrameworkBundle among others. The package is developed by Paweł Jędrzejewski, Sylius Project contributors, and the community.
