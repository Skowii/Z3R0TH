1. The tutorial(s) don't already exist.
2. Tutorial is under correct language/technology.
3. Improvements of the same tutorial are welcome.

7. Use the specified format for titles and links.
8. Check spelling, grammar, and follow CONTRIBUTING file.
9. Remove trailing whitespaces and use Google URL Shortener if needed.
