"30 seconds of code" is a website that offers coding articles to help developers improve their skills. Visitors can search for relevant articles and collections based on name, tags, language, or description. They can browse through articles and individual collections covering various topics. Each card provides the full article with code snippets, explanations, and examples. To contribute, users can report problems or suggest improvements.
