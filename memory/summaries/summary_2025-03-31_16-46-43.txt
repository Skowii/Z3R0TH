The Sylius product library is a product catalog system with support for product options and variants. It is used for ecommerce, shops, and assortment management. The project is licensed under the MIT license and has contributors including Paweł Jędrzejewski, the Sylius project, and community contributions. It requires PHP 7.4 or 8.0 and various dependencies such as Behat/Transliterator and Symfony components.
