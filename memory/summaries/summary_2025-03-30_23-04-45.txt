This article discusses the importance of print stylesheets for presenting website content in a legible and print-friendly manner. It provides a simple, opinionated print stylesheet as a base for users to customize their own print styling. The stylesheet uses the `@media print` query to apply specific styles when printing, such as removing margins and padding, setting fonts and font sizes, making headings bold, adding links with URL after them, resizing images, and hiding unnecessary page elements like headers, footers, and navigation.
