To submit an agent to the AutoGPT Marketplace:
1. Build a completed agent using the AutoGPT Builder and save it.
2. Access the Submission Form by navigating to the marketplace and clicking "Submit Agent."
3. Select your agent from the dropdown menu.
4. Fill out required information, including a description, author name, keywords, and category.
5. Review and agree to the marketplace terms.
6. Click "Submit" to complete the submission.
7. The AutoGPT team will review your submission, after which your agent will appear in the marketplace as a new listing if approved.
