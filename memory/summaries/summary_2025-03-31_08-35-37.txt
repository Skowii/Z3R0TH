The project aims to foster an open and welcoming community, with a commitment to making participation free from harassment for everyone regardless of factors such as gender or ethnicity. Unacceptable behavior includes sexualized language, personal attacks, trolling, and other unprofessional conduct. Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to enforce this code by removing or rejecting contributions that don't align with it. This code applies in both project spaces and public spaces when representing the project. Complaints will be reviewed and investigated, with confidentiality maintained for the reporter.
