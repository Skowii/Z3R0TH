The Sylius Currency Bundle is a Symfony bundle that processes currencies for use within Symfony applications. It includes features such as cash management, currency conversion, and money handling, while also providing compatibility with the Sylius resource bundle and Symfony framework bundle. The package requires PHP 7.4 or 8.0, and is licensed under the MIT license. Its authors include Paweł Jędrzejewski, the Sylius project, and community contributions.
