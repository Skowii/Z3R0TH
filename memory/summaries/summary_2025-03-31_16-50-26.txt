The Sylius Payment Bundle is a flexible payments system for Symfony e-commerce applications. It enables managing various payment methods, such as credit card, bank transfer, or online wallets, and supports multiple currencies. The bundle requires PHP version 7.4 or 8.0 and is compatible with Symfony framework versions 4.4 or 5.2. The package is licensed under the MIT license and has contributors from Sylius project and the community.
