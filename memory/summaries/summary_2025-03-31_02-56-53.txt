8 tips for working from home:
1. Set up a well-designed workspace
2. Establish ground rules with roommates or significant others
3. Inform others of your availability
4. Socialize with coworkers to prevent feelings of isolation
5. Maintain professionalism in appearance and behavior
6. Plan daily and weekly tasks for better time management
7. Prioritize important tasks and communicate progress with team members
8. Get dressed appropriately if joining video calls
