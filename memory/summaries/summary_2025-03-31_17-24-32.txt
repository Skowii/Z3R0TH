The "Gmail Read" block retrieves and reads emails from a specified Gmail account based on search criteria, providing information such as subject, sender, recipient, date, content, and attachments. The "Gmail Send" block allows users to compose and send emails using their Gmail account. The "Gmail List Labels" block retrieves all labels (categories) from a Gmail account for organizing emails. The "Gmail Add Label" block adds a label to a specific email in a Gmail account, creating the label if it doesn't exist already.
