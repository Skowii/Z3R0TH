The AutoGPT Contribution Guide provides information on how to contribute to the project. All contributions to the autogpt_platform folder are under a Contribution License Agreement, while other folders use the MIT license. To contribute, avoid duplicate work and issues, collaborate with others, create draft PRs for bigger changes, follow code guidelines, and test your changes before submitting a PR. Additionally, consider contributing in ways other than code. The project also encourages involvement through Catalyzing efforts and joining their Discord community.
