Sylius Core Bundle is a Symfony bundle that integrates all other bundles into a full stack ecommerce solution. It supports shop, ecommerce, store, and webshop functionalities. The bundle requires PHP 7.4 or 8.0, and includes various dependencies such as Doctrine Migrations Bundle, Email Validator, FakerPHP, JMS Serializer-Bundle, Liip Imagine-Bundle, among others.
