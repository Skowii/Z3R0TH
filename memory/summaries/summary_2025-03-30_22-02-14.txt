A hybrid business model combines a physical fitness center with an AI-driven automation office to manage multiple online income streams. The gym offers AI-powered equipment, VR workout stations, biometric entry, and smart recovery services for $99-$299/month per member, with additional subscription packages available. The AI office upstairs supports passive income through trading, content creation, e-commerce, software development, lead generation, and consulting. The business aims to generate a total monthly revenue of $1M-$5M+, targeting gym enthusiasts, entrepreneurs, athletes, corporate wellness clients, fitness influencers, and tech-savvy members.
