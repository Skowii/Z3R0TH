Accessible websites are more user-friendly and beneficial in attracting a diverse audience. Here are eight tips to improve website accessibility:

1. Use semantic HTML for a better understanding of the layout and its elements.
2. Ensure proper color usage by adhering to contrast ratio requirements and using colors meaningfully.
3. Caption images and video content for screenreaders and users with visual impairments.
4. Utilize icons, colors, and text literals together to convey information to all users.
5. Maintain consistency in design elements, especially buttons and links.
6. Label your input fields with appropriate labels or attributes to ensure clarity for screenreaders.
7. Design responsively by catering to various devices and navigation methods.
8. Organize content effectively to enhance the user experience across all platforms and users.
