In this New Year's update, the author shares their plans for the upcoming year. They will be allowing community contributions on GitHub again, focusing on bug fixes, typos, and small improvements to maintain alignment with their vision. A new discussion feature has been added for community feedback on articles. The About page has been updated with more personal information and content from the FAQ page merged into it. A journeys feature has been added, making it easier to follow along with article series in collections. Finally, terminology is being clarified to differentiate between articles and snippets.
