The Sylius Taxation Bundle is a flexible taxation system designed for Symfony ecommerce applications. It has keywords related to tax, taxes, and taxation as well as ecommerce, shop, and webshop. The bundle requires PHP 7.4 or 8.0, various other dependencies, and the Symfony framework bundle. It also provides a developer-friendly environment with numerous tools and tests for efficient development. The homepage is https://sylius.com and it is licensed under MIT.
