AutoGPT Forge is a ready-to-go template for building your own AI agent application, allowing you to focus on the unique aspects of your agent without dealing with boilerplate code. It provides brain-centric development, tools for efficient component integration, and compatibility with top AI tools. To get started, fork or download the AutoGPT repository, review the example agent in `classic/forge/agent/forge_agent.py`, and explore built-in components in `classic/forge/components/`. Outdated tutorial series is available for further guidance on setting up and building your project.
