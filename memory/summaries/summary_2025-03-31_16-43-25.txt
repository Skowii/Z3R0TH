The Sylius/Channel library is a PHP component that helps manage different sales channels for e-commerce purposes. It supports multichannel, multistore, and e-commerce functionalities. It is developed by Paweł Jędrzejewski, the Sylius project, and community contributors. Requirements include PHP 7.4 or 8.0, Sylius/Resource, Symfony/Form, Symfony/HttpFoundation, and Laminas/Laminas-Stdlib. The library is licensed under MIT and can be found at https://sylius.com.
