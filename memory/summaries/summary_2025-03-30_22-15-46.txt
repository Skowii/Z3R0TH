The Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct pledges to create a harassment-free environment for everyone regardless of various factors, promoting openness, diversity, and inclusivity. Behaviors contributing to a positive community environment include empathy, respecting opinions, accepting feedback, and focusing on the overall community's best interests. Unacceptable behavior includes sexual language, trolling, harassment, publishing private information, or any other inappropriate conduct. Community leaders are responsible for enforcing these standards, investigating incidents, and maintaining confidentiality of reporters.
