The Sylius Taxonomy Bundle is a flexible categorization system for Symfony applications, useful in ecommerce environments. It allows for categorizing and sorting products or services into custom categories and taxonomies. This bundle requires PHP version 7.4 or 8.0, and several other dependencies including the Twig templating engine, Doctrine extensions, and the Sylius resource and core bundles.
