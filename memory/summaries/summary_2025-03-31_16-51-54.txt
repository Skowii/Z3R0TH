The Sylius Shipping Bundle is a flexible shipping system designed for Symfony ecommerce applications. It includes features such as shipping, delivery, shipments, shop, webshop, and ecommerce functionalities. The bundle requires PHP version 7.4 or 8.0, various other dependencies and libraries, and relies on the Sylius Shipping core component. The project is maintained by multiple authors, including the original creator Paweł Jędrzejewski, the Sylius project team, and community contributors.
