AutoGPT is a groundbreaking platform that enables users to create, deploy and manage continuous agents for automating workflows. The platform's architecture consists of two main components: the AutoGPT Server and the AutoGPT Frontend. Users can design their AI agents by creating customized workflows with building blocks representing actions such as connections to external services, data processing tools, and AI models. AutoGPT comes pre-integrated with cutting-edge language model providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, and Llama. The platform adopts a dual-license approach that encourages collaboration while supporting sustainable development.
