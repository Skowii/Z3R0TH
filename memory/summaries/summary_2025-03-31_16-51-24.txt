The Sylius Promotion Bundle is a Symfony bundle that allows for managing ecommerce promotions in a Symfony application. It supports features such as coupons, discounts, and promotions. The bundle's keywords include 'shop', 'ecommerce', 'promotions', 'coupons', 'discounts', and 'sylius'. It is compatible with PHP 7.4 or 8.0 and uses the Doctrine Extensions Bundle. The authors of this bundle are Paweł Jędrzejewski, Sylius project contributors, and community members.
