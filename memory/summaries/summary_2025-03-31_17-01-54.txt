Sylius Shop Bundle is a Symfony bundle designed for eCommerce storefronts. It requires PHP ^7.4 || ^8.0 and has a homepage at https://sylius.com. The project is licensed under the MIT license, with contributors including Paweł Jędrzejewski and Community contributions. Requirements include Sylius Core Bundle, Sylius UI Bundle, Symfony Framework Bundle, and Symfony Security Bundle, along with various dependencies for testing and development.
