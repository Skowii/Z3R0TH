This is a description of the sylius/channel-bundle, a Symfony bundle that handles currencies and channel formatting. It requires PHP 7.4 or 8.0, and includes various dependencies like Sylius/resource-bundle and Symfony/framework-bundle. The bundle was developed by Paweł Jędrzejewski, the Sylius project, and community contributions. The software is licensed under MIT and has test files in the "test/app" directory.
