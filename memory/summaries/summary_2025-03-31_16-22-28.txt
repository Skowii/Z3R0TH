This is a curriculum for Siraj Raval's "Learn Machine Learning in 3 Months" video on YouTube. The content is divided into three months of study. In the first month, topics include linear algebra, calculus, probability, and algorithms. The second month focuses on learning Python for data science, math of intelligence, and introductory TensorFlow. Month two also includes an introduction to machine learning from Udacity. The third month is dedicated to deep learning, with resources such as an introduction to the topic from YouTube, Deep Learning by Fast.AI, and re-implementation of projects from a specific GitHub page. Additional resources include following prominent people in ML on Twitter and joining the "Wizards" Slack channel.
