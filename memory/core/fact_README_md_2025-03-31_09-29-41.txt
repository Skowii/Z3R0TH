Auto-GPT Benchmarks is a tool for objectively measuring the performance of various AI models and agents in categories like code, retrieval, memory, and safety. It automates benchmarking processes to save time and money, enabling users to evaluate their agent's capabilities. Currently, Beebot ranks first, followed by mini-agi and Auto-GPT.
