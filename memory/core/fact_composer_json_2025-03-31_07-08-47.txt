Addressing and zone management bundle for Symfony applications. Includes address, addressing, and zones keywords. Requirements include PHP 7.4 or 8.0, stof/doctrine-extensions-bundle, sylius/addressing, and other Sylius bundles and libraries. Supports development with doctrine/orm, phpspec/phpspec, phpunit/phpunit, and symfony dependencies. The package supports Symfony 4.4 or 5.2 and has a minimum stability of dev.
